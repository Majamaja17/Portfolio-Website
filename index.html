About me

My adventure with programming began in early 2018, but the preparation and curiosity associated with this professional area had their beginning a few years earlier. In 2016, I completed a graphic design course. I have learned good web design practices and have completed several commercial print material orders. Then I already knew that in my future work I would like to participate in the process of creating websites. I decided to use the acquired knowledge also in internet marketing. I worked on creating and developing employer websites. I served social media profiles and prepared effective mailings.

The decisive step for me to look for a job as Frontend Developerwas to implement the Bootcamp Web Developer - Kodilla course.In parallel to gaining substantive knowledge covering the scope of the course, I got to know the IT environment, the philosophy of the team of programmers, as well as the way of thinking and overcoming the challenges emerging in the daily work of Frontend Developers.
